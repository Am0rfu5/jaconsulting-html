Universities & Non-Profits

Empowering Non-Profits: Advanced web solutions and data integration for renowned organizations like Aspen Institute and Yale University.

Our work with non-profit organizations spans a wide range of technological advancements and solutions tailored to meet the unique needs of each entity.

We served as architect and lead developer for web applications, CMS, and data integrations. This included entire life-cycle from inception to launch of the member communications portal for the world-renowned leadership and social welfare organization, the Aspen Global Leadership Network.

For AGLN, we designed and implemented customizations and interfaces, integrating the legacy CRM database (Raiserâ€™s Edge) using RESTful web services. We created a flexible Content Subscription Email System offering members personalized filters, and developed a comprehensive dashboard system. This system enabled Fellows (users) to create and search content, including job and investment opportunities, and manage an events system with registration capabilities.

We also focused on creating detailed documentation for administration and content creation for AGLN, ensuring sustainability and ease of use for the organization.

Our involvement extended to the Pratt Institute for Community Development, where we developed, launched, and maintained their website, PrattCenter.net. Additionally, we undertook development work on the Yale University Environmental Performance Index site (epi.yale.edu) and the Columbia University Global Commons site.

For over 15 years, we have built, rebuilt, and maintained a full-featured membership website for ORRA, Inc., the Oriental Rug Retailers of America. This included the capability for profile editing and providing support for back-office and communication technologies.

Lastly, our expertise was applied in engineering and deploying sites onto LAMP-based development, staging, and production servers, utilizing Git version control. This ensured robust, secure, and efficient deployment of each project.

In summary, our engagement with non-profit clients has been a journey of delivering advanced web solutions, data integration, and system development, significantly enhancing the digital capabilities of these esteemed organizations.
